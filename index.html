Discover Sri Lanka’s Wonders

Explore the island's most iconic destinations, each with its own unique story, beauty, and experiences.

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

Ancient royal citadel (5th century) with breathtaking panoramic views from the top of a 200-meter rock plateau.

Buy

Handicrafts, batik, wooden masks

Eat

King coconut, rice & curry

Ella

Hill town known for tea fields, waterfalls, and the iconic Nine Arches Bridge. Perfect for hiking and photography.

Buy

Ceylon tea, handmade souvenirs

Eat

Hoppers, kottu roti, tropical juice

Kandy

Cultural capital of Sri Lanka, home to the sacred Temple of the Tooth and beautiful botanical gardens.

Buy

Gems, spices, traditional crafts

Eat

Lamprais, milk rice, jackfruit curry

Galle

UNESCO World Heritage site with colonial architecture, lighthouse, and charming cobblestone streets.

Buy

Lace, antiques, cinnamon products

Eat

Seafood, Dutch-influenced cuisine

Mirissa

Paradise beach famous for whale watching, palm-fringed shores, and vibrant beach parties.

Buy

Beachwear, coconut oil products

Eat

Fresh seafood, tropical cocktails

Dambulla

Golden Temple with five caves containing over 150 statues and ancient Buddhist murals.

Buy

Brassware, religious artifacts

Eat

Vegetable rotis, wood apple juice
